Overview

On Thursday 29th June 2017 at 7pm, Dragons Running Club (Sale) are proud to host our first ever race.
An easy going, summertime, evening 5 mile trail race through the woodlands and along the river banks of the River Mersey.
The race will be held at Metroviks Rugby Club in Sale and will have a glorious start and finish on the raised banks of the River Mersey.

All profits will be shared with local community youth groups/charities.

As of 18th June 2017 this race is now FULL. If you are still interested email us at traffordtrails@gmail.com and we will add you to our reserve list.

Please consider the following before racing


If you in any doubt on the day about your ability to complete the 5 mile race, then you are advised not to start.
If you are new to running and are considering entering the race, you are advised to consult a doctor regarding your wish to take up running and racing as a form of exercise.
It is recommended that you wear appropriate trail footwear, however, if you feel comfortable running on trails in road specific footwear then that is you choice.

We operate 5 simple rules:
1. Look after yourself
2. Look after others
3. Look after the environment
4. Try your damned hardest and
5. Enjoy the race!
