Overview

On Thursday 29th June 2017 at 7pm, Dragons Running Club (Sale) are proud to host our first ever race.
An easy going, summertime, evening 5 mile trail race through the woodlands and along the river banks of the river mersey.
The race will be held at Metroviks Rugby Club in Sale and will have a glorious start and finish on the raised banks of the river mersey.

All profits to be shared with community youth groups/charities.

Info for competitors


All competitors must be in a fit state to undertake the 5 mile trail run as described.
If competitors are in any doubt of their ability to complete a 5 mile run, they will be advised not to start.
If competitors are new to running, they are advised to get a check-up from a doctor indicating that they would like to take up running and racing as a form of exercise.
It is recommended that competitors wear appropriate trail footwear, however if the competitor feels comfortable running on trails in road specific footwear then that is their choice.

We operate 4 simple rules:
1. Look after yourself
2. Look after others
3. Look after the environment
4. Try your damned hardest and
5. Enjoy the race!
